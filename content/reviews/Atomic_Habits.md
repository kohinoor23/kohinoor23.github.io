# _A-tom-ic Hab-its_

## The Fundamentals
> Why Tiny Changes Make a Big Difference

### The Surprising Power of Atomic Habits
$\text{Outcome} = f(\text{Preparedness})$  
- If we improve ourselves `1%` each day, we grow better by `37` times. 

    Similarly, if we degrade ourselves bit-by-bit, we can end up being a nobody, a mere `3%` of our old-selves.

- Understand the `Plateau of Latent Potential`, a.k.a The Valley of Disappointment. Growth is slow at first, but breathtaking over time. These figures illustrate the same.
[Insert2Plots]

- Forget about, and focus on `Systems` instead. Live the process. Goal-based working has serious issues:  
  - Winners and Losers have same goals.
  - Achieving a goal is only a momentary change
  - Goals restrict our happiness.
  - Goals are at odds with long-term progress.

### How Our Habits Shape Our Identity (and Vice Versa)
Any behavioural change can occur at three levels
- Outcomes
- Processes
- Identity

Outcome-based habits tend to go top to bottom, and struggle to bring about a change. What we want are `Identity-based` habits.

Eg. ~~I want to go to gym.~~ I am a gymmer.

The motivation becomes more intrinsic as we go deep the levels.

When we see ourselves in bad light, we enforce identity-based bad habits. Eg. I am bad at talking to girls. I am not a logical person. _Keep this in mind next time you curse yourself_.
